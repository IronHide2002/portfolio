## Typography Hierarchy

### Playfair Display (Serif) - Headings
- **Character**: Elegant, classic, sophisticated
- **Impact**: High contrast, dramatic serifs
- **Use cases**: All headings, titles, names
- **Weights**: 400-900 available

### Space Grotesk (Sans-Serif) - Body
- **Character**: Modern, geometric, clean
- **Readability**: Excellent for body text
- **Use cases**: Descriptions, bullets, UI text
- **Weights**: 300-700

### The Perfect Pairing
This combination creates:
- **Editorial quality**: Like premium magazines
- **Professional polish**: Sophisticated without being stuffy
- **Clear hierarchy**: Instant visual distinction
- **Modern classic**: Timeless yet contemporary
